  • NewVision (2022)

    As the global pandemic drew to a close, the limitations faced by disabled individuals, particularly the visually impaired, became glaringly apparent in our daily lives. Motivated by a desire to address these challenges, we embarked on the development of an electronic device aimed at enhancing accessibility and safety. The project comprised three key stages: sensor implementation, processing facilitated by a discrete electronic circuit, and the integration of an alert system to notify users of potential hazards. Collaborating closely with the Asociacion Argentina de Ciegos (A.S.A.C.), we were able to leverage their expertise and insights to ensure the device met the specific needs of the visually impaired community.

  • Ecodesafio (2023)

My role focused on designing and implementing sensor circuits and power electronics, while my colleagues focused on creating a neural network to analyze the data I collected.
